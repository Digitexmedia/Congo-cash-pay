Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is a virtual Visa card?

A virtual Visa card is a digital version of a physical card that can be used to pay online, subscribe, or make global purchases securely.

2. How much does a card cost?

Each card costs $10, paid securely via PayPal. You’ll receive your card instantly after payment.

3. Where can I use the card?

You can use it on most international websites that accept Visa — like Netflix, Amazon, Facebook Ads, and more.

4. Do I need to verify my identity (KYC)?

Yes. You must submit KYC with ID and live face scan before your first card is activated.

5. How do I receive my card?

After your PayPal payment, your virtual Visa card details (number, expiry, CVV) will be delivered to your dashboard or email automatically.

6. Can I reload or reuse the card?

No. The cards are single-use and disposable for better security. Buy a new card anytime you need.
